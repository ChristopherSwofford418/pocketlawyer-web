Subscription & Billing

PocketLawyer Pro subscriptions are billed through the Apple App Store. To manage, pause, or cancel your subscription, go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone. Apple handles all billing; we do not store payment information.

Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, email us at privacy@pocketlawyer.io with the subject line "Delete My Account." We will process your request within 30 days.

Legal Disclaimer

PocketLawyer provides AI-generated legal information for educational purposes only. It does not constitute legal advice and does not create an attorney-client relationship. For advice specific to your situation, please consult a licensed attorney in your jurisdiction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PocketLawyer a real lawyer?
No. PocketLawyer is an AI-powered tool that provides general legal information. It is not a licensed attorney and cannot represent you in court.

How do I cancel my subscription?
Cancel anytime through your iPhone's Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → PocketLawyer.

Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. See our Privacy Policy for details.
